About Me

My name is Sehrish Hassan. I am currently a graphic design student at the College of Dupage. Although my major is Graphic Design, my hobby and my passion are in makeup. I like spending much of my free time watching makeup tutorials, doing makeup, and posting my own makeup tutorials online. My dream for the future is to mix the skills I have acquired through Graphic Design with my makeup, to create my own branding.

My Style

Throughout my time as a student of Graphic Designing at the College of Dupage, I have tried various different styles and techniques. When I realized I can mix my interest in doing henna tattoos with graphic designing, I started illustrating designs based on traditional henna patterns. From there, I started digitally painting in photoshop. Today, I enjoy using my tablet to make digital illustrations that are detailed and full of life.
