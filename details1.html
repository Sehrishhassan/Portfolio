Paneerish



This project required me to create a brand for a food truck. I chose to create my own company, named "Paneerish", which would serve paneer dishes. Paneer is an indian cheese, and is typically vegetarian.

I had to design the logo myself. After trying out multiple designs, I decided a fork with a paneer cube on it was the most fitting. Since indian cuisine is typically spicy, the flame was an added touch that made the logo stand out.

Next, I had to design the food truck itself. The pattern seen on the food truck was also designed by me.

Finally, for my implementation, I decided some take out containers were the best to show how the packaging from Paneerish would look like.

Overall, this was a tough assignment because there was so much that we had to do. But it was exciting to be able to create my own branding.
